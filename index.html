What is Global warming ?

Global Warming is a change in the earth's temperature and weather patterns.These changes used to be natural but ever since the 1800s humans have also impacted this change mainly because of burning fossil fules wich produces heat-trapping gases.In the rest of this page you will learn how global warming affects in diffrent places.

Purpouse

The purpouse of this page is to inform you as a reader how global warming is constantly affecting the world around us.

Global Warming P.R.

How Puerto Rico is affected by Global warming:

  1. Global Warming can cause longer dry periods of time.

  2. It causes more hurricanes in our area.

  3. The increase in heat in the area causes sea leveles to continue to rise.

Global Warming U.S.

How the United States is affected by Global Warming:

  1. Global Warming in the U.S. has caused an increase in wild fires.

  2. It has also decreased water supplies.

  3. Healths are impacted by the heat.

Global Warming World

How the world is affected by Global warming:

  1. It causes many alternations to the enviornment.

  2. It is a threat to biodiversity.

  3. It causes a rise in sea leveles.

  4. It increases the risk in droughts.

What to do

What can we do to help stop Global Warming:

  1. We can help by reducing the amount of energy we use in our homes.

  2. We can make more use of the three R's ( reuse, reduce, recycle).

  3. We can prevent using cars and use bikes or walk if places are near.
  4. Talk to people about Global Warming and tell them how it is affecting us.
